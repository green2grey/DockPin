DockPin

Pin your Mac's Dock to specific displays and prevent it from jumping between screens.

Download for Mac
Features
🔒
Lock the Dock to selected displays
🖥
Allow or disallow specific monitors
⌥
Override locking with a modifier key
⚡
Launch at startup with persistent settings
○
Lightweight menu bar app — no Dock icon, runs silently in the background
Requirements
  • macOS 13 or later
  • Two or more displays
  • Dock at the bottom of the screen
